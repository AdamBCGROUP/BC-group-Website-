Company Profile

New Zealand owned and operated since 1992, BC Group has built a reputation as the country's most trusted compliance partner.

Who We Are

Over 30 years of building compliance expertise

BC Group (Building Compliance Group Ltd) was established in 1992 to specialise in building systems inspection and reporting. What began as a small operation has grown into a trusted compliance management company with support staff based in Auckland, Christchurch, and Melbourne.

We act as the bridge between building owners, IQP contractors, and local councils — managing the full Building Warrant of Fitness process so our clients can focus on what they do best.

Our online BWOF Management System gives clients, councils, and contractors 24/7 access to current building records, inspection schedules, and compliance documentation from anywhere in the world.

Our Mission

To deliver transparent, reliable, and personalised compliance management that gives building owners total confidence in their obligations under the Building Act 2004.

How We Work

Personalised service, built around you

At BC Group, we believe compliance shouldn't be complicated. Every client's building portfolio is different, and we tailor our support to meet your specific needs rather than applying a one-size-fits-all approach.

We build long-term partnerships with our clients, providing consistent, responsive service backed by strong relationships with IQP contractors, local councils, and regulatory bodies across New Zealand.

  • Dedicated support staff for every client
  • Customised compliance management plans
  • Proactive communication and deadline management
  • Strong contractor and council relationships
  • Fully insured with $10M professional indemnity cover

Our Credentials

Professional, insured, and accountable

BC Group holds $10 million in professional indemnity and public liability insurance coverage, giving our clients complete confidence that their compliance obligations are in safe hands.
