Digital Log Book

Ensure your buildings are maintained correctly with our Digital Log Book, powered by SiteConnect.

Powered by SiteConnect

A smarter way to manage site activity

BC Group has partnered with SiteConnect — the emerging leader in Site Management and Health & Safety Software — to deliver a Digital Log Book solution for building owners and property managers.

The Digital Log Book records all site activity in one place, giving you a clear, auditable record of who has been on site, what systems have been maintained, and what remedial work is required.

Visitor Records

Records the details of every visitor and contractor who accesses the site, creating a complete audit trail.

System Maintenance Tracking

Identifies which specified systems are being maintained and captures the IQP number for each inspection.

Remedial Item Logging

Documents any remedial items identified during inspections so nothing falls through the cracks.

Cloud-Based & Accessible

All records are stored securely in the cloud and accessible anytime, from any device.
