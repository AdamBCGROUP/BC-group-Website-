What We Do

Comprehensive Compliance Services

We manage the full Building Warrant of Fitness process on your behalf — from coordinating inspections to submitting annual documentation to your local council.

BWOF Management

We manage your Building Warrant of Fitness end to end — coordinating IQP inspections, collecting reports, and submitting all documentation to council on time.

Learn more

IQP Inspections

Our network of qualified IQP contractors conduct inspections of all specified systems — from fire alarms and sprinklers to lifts, air conditioning and final exits.

Learn more

Document Management

All compliance documents are stored securely on our online platform, accessible 24/7 by building owners, councils, contractors, and inspectors worldwide.

Learn more

Digital Log Book

Powered by SiteConnect, our Digital Log Book records visitor details, system maintenance activity, IQP numbers, and remedial items for each property.

Learn more

Administrative Support

We coordinate and collect annual contractor reports, Form 12A documents, and issue annual IQP reports and Building Warrant of Fitness certificates on time.

Learn more

Evacuation Services

We provide evacuation specialist services, including the issuing of annual evacuation trial reports to ensure your building meets all fire safety requirements.

Learn more

Why BC Group

Over 30 years of compliance expertise

Since 1992, BC Group has built a reputation for reliability, accuracy, and personalised service. We act as the bridge between building owners, IQP contractors, and local councils — removing the complexity of compliance from your plate.

  • Full BWOF process management from start to finish
  • IQP contractor subcontracting across all local regions
  • Inspections conducted under strict quality management systems
  • $10 million professional indemnity and public liability insurance
  • Online system with 24/7 access to all building records
  • Support staff based in Auckland, Christchurch, and Melbourne
