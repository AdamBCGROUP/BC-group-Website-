Secure, real-time access to all your building compliance records — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, from anywhere in the world.

What the Portal Does

Everything in one place

BC Group's web-based compliance management system stores all your BWOF documentation securely in one place. Clients, contractors, councils, and inspectors all have secure, role-based access to real-time building records.

  • Secure online document storage under the Building Act 2004
  • 24/7 access for clients, councils, contractors, and inspectors
  • Inspection scheduling and online recording
  • Report upload and version control
  • Automated email notifications for compliance issues
  • Staff action notes and communication logs
