Our Services

From BWOF management to IQP inspections and digital compliance tools, we handle every aspect of building compliance on your behalf.

Building Warrant of Fitness Management

The Building Warrant of Fitness (BWOF) process requires building owners to ensure all specified systems are inspected, maintained, and documented annually. BC Group manages this entire process on your behalf.

We coordinate with your IQP contractors, collect all required reports and Form 12A certificates, and submit your annual BWOF documentation to the local council — accurately and on time, every time.

What we manage for you:

  • Compliance schedule management and monitoring
  • IQP contractor coordination and subcontracting
  • Collection of annual reports and Form 12A certificates
  • Annual BWOF certificate issuance
  • Submission of documentation to local councils
  • Ongoing communication with building owners

Building Owner Obligations

Under the Building Act 2004, building owners must:

  • Obtain and display a compliance schedule
  • Engage qualified IQPs for inspections
  • Collect Form 12A inspection certificates
  • Submit annual BWOF documentation to council
  • Display the BWOF in a public area of the building

BC Group handles all of this for you.

IQP Inspection Services

BC Group has an established network of qualified IQP (Independent Qualified Person) contractors across New Zealand. We subcontract inspections to experienced local professionals, ensuring your specified systems are inspected to the highest standard.

All inspections are conducted under documented quality management systems, providing consistent and accurate results across your entire building portfolio.

Specified systems we cover:

  • Final exits and means of escape
  • Fire and smoke separation systems
  • Signage and emergency lighting
  • Automatic fire sprinkler systems
  • Fire alarm systems
  • Backflow prevention devices
  • Lifts and escalators
  • Air conditioning and mechanical ventilation

Quality You Can Trust

Every inspection conducted through BC Group is carried out by an IQP registered under the relevant authority. Our quality management systems ensure every report is accurate, complete, and submitted on time.


BC Group holds $10 million in professional indemnity and public liability insurance, giving you total peace of mind.

Online Document Management

BC Group's web-based compliance management system stores all your BWOF documentation securely in one place. Clients, contractors, councils, and inspectors all have secure, role-based access to real-time building records — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, from anywhere in the world.

Platform features:

  • 24/7 access for clients, councils, contractors, and inspectors

Evacuation Specialist Services

Fire evacuation compliance is a critical component of building safety. BC Group provides evacuation specialist services, ensuring your evacuation schemes are maintained and your buildings meet all fire safety obligations.

What's included:

  • Evacuation scheme review and management
  • Annual evacuation trial coordination
  • Issuing of annual evacuation trial reports
  • Liaison with New Zealand Fire and Emergency
  • Documentation and record keeping

Why It Matters

Buildings that require an evacuation scheme under the Fire and Emergency New Zealand Act must have it approved and kept current. Failure to comply can result in significant penalties for building owners.


BC Group ensures your evacuation obligations are met — on time, every year.
